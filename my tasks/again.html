Margaret Hamilton

American computer scientist

A Short Biography of Margaret Hamilton:

  • Hamilton was born August 17, 1936, in Indian, U.S.
  • She studied University of Michigan
  • She earned BA in mathematics with a minor philosophy
  • Hamilton developed software for predicting weather on LGP-30
  • She workes for SAGE and wrote software to search unfriendly aircraft
  • SAGE was soon developed for military use in anti-aircraft air defense
  • She was the first programmer and fisrt woman in Apollo Progect
  • Later she became Director of Software engineering division, Apollo, NASA
  • In 2016 she received the Presidential Medal of Freedom from Barack Obama
  • She was married two times
  • She has a daughter
  • - Now, she is business owner

  • She cofounded the company "Hamilton Technologies"
  • She also received NASA's Exceptional Space Act Award
